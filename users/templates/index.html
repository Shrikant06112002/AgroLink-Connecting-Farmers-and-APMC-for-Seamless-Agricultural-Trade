About
Kisan Sathi
A bridge between farmers and APMC (Agricultural Product Market Committee), is a application developed to assist farmers to sell their commodities efficiently without wasting much time in long queues.
Farmers had to spend a lot of time travelling to market yards and selling their products, and there is much agricultural loss due to adverse weather conditions during this period. To address this issue, this platform is build that will serve as a link between farmers and market yards, allowing farmers to enhance agricultural output by minimising time spent waiting at market yards and selling crop items there.
